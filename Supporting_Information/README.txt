This directory contains (besides this README.txt) four folders (Fibril, Monomer, Collagen, Fibronectin) that contain files with the atomic coordinates (in PDB format) of aS structures discussed in the article.

Fibril;
This folder contains five sub-directories containing all SAA3 fibril configurations:

1) control:
Atomic coordinates (in PDB format) of the start and final configurations of the control SAA3 fibril simulations:
saa3_f_control_1_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril replica 1
saa3_f_control_2_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril replica 2
saa3_f_control_3_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril replica 3
saa3_f_control_1_final   : Final structure of SAA3 fibril replica 1
saa3_f_control_2_final   : Final structure of SAA3 fibril replica 2
saa3_f_control_3_final   : Final structure of SAA3 fibril replica 3

2) R5S:
Atomic coordinates (in PDB format) of the start and final configurations of the SAA3 fibril simulations with R5S:
saa3_f_R5S_1_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril with R5S replica 1
saa3_f_R5S_2_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril with R5S replica 2

saa3_f_R5S_1_final   : Final structure of SAA3 fibril with R5S replica 1
saa3_f_R5S_2_final   : Final structure of SAA3 fibril with R5S replica 2

saa3_f_G6F_2_initial : Initial structure of SAA3 fibril with G6F replica 2
